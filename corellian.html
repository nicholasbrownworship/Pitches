The Corellian Gambit

Pitch

The crew is hired to sabotage Imperial operations on Corellia. Espionage, infiltration, and political manipulation dominate as they navigate Imperial officers, rival crime syndicates, and Rebel contacts. Their actions may determine Corellia’s future.

Player Hooks

  • Spy or operative with Rebel connections
  • Hacker/slicer skilled at infiltration
  • Pilot with fast ships
  • Con artist or diplomat manipulating factions
  • Force-sensitive lurking in shadows

Campaign Arc

  • Short missions → uncover Imperial plot
  • Player decisions shape Corellia’s politics
  • Optional Force-sensitive subplots
