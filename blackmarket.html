Black Market Wars

Pitch

The crew is drawn into a war between Outer Rim crime syndicates after a high-value shipment goes missing. Betrayal, negotiation, and combat determine who dominates, while the Empire and local gangs complicate the crew’s path to survival and profit.

Player Hooks

  • Smuggler/pilot with underworld ties
  • Bounty hunter protecting or recovering the shipment
  • Soldier/mercenary enforcing or guarding
  • Slicer or con artist manipulating rivals
  • Rebel contact aiming to weaken syndicates

Campaign Arc

  • Recovering or stealing shipment → escalating gang wars
  • Crew gains influence, attracts attention
  • Player choices determine which factions survive or fall
