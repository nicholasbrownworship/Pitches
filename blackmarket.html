Black Market Wars

The Galactic Civil War stretches across the stars, but battles aren’t fought only with fleets and armies. In the criminal underworld, wars rage over spice shipments, stolen weapons, and illegal technology. Rival syndicates fight for dominance, while the Empire cracks down on smugglers, and the Rebel Alliance tries to turn the chaos to their advantage. The players are caught in the middle, whether by choice or circumstance.

The Premise

As a crew of scoundrels, smugglers, and fixers, the party is drawn into a growing conflict between syndicates whose turf wars spill into Imperial space. Each deal, shipment, and betrayal can shift the balance of power. In this game, the crew must decide who to ally with, who to betray, and how far they’re willing to go to profit — or survive — in a galaxy where crime is as dangerous as the Empire itself.

Why Play This Campaign

This campaign focuses on the gritty underworld of the Original Trilogy era, perfect for characters who thrive on cunning, charm, and a willingness to bend the rules. Every session will bring tough choices about loyalty, greed, and survival, with credits and consequences always on the line. If you’ve ever wanted to be part of the syndicate wars that run parallel to the fight for the galaxy, this is your story.
