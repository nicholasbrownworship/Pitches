Black Market Wars

The Galactic Civil War isn’t fought only with fleets and armies — it’s also waged in the shadows of the underworld. Smuggling routes, illegal weapons caches, and spice shipments can tilt entire battles. Rival syndicates fight for dominance, while the Empire seeks to crush them and the Rebellion seeks to use them. The players find themselves in the middle of this cutthroat economic war, forced to navigate loyalties and betrayals while carving out their own fortune.
