Crimson Tides

Pitch

The crew gets caught in a violent Outer Rim pirate war. Smuggling, treasure hunting, and double-crosses define a chaotic struggle for survival. Loyalties shift constantly, and the crew may become the rulers—or victims—of the pirate underworld.

Player Hooks

  • Pirate or ex-privateer seeking revenge or fortune
  • Smuggler or slicer with personal scores
  • Bounty hunter with moral code
  • Force-sensitive hiding in the Outer Rim
  • Mercenary/ex-military for tactics and muscle

Campaign Arc

  • Local conflicts escalate into full pirate war
  • Player alliances define power dynamics
  • Imperial or Rebel interference raises stakes
