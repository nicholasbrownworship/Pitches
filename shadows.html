Shadows Over Ryloth

Pitch

A ragtag crew of smugglers uncovers a dangerous secret on Ryloth, drawing the attention of rival factions, bounty hunters, and Imperial forces. Survival and profit are only the beginning; their choices will shape the fate of the local systems.

Player Hooks

  • Smuggler or thief navigating the underworld
  • Ex-Imperial with secrets to hide
  • Pilot with Rebel contacts
  • Force-sensitive operating in secret
  • Mercenary bounty hunter with a personal code

Campaign Arc

  • Small jobs escalate into factional intrigue
  • Player choices shape local politics and power struggles
  • Optional Force-sensitive threads can be included
