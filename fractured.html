The Fractured Republic

Pitch

Political instability and corruption plague a Mid Rim system. The crew is hired to mediate disputes, protect key figures, or sabotage schemes. Each choice ripples through planetary politics and Imperial oversight, giving players tangible impact on the region’s fate.

Player Hooks

  • Diplomat or con artist
  • Soldier/mercenary offering protection
  • Rebel sympathizer or spy
  • Force-sensitive acting in secret
  • Smuggler or pilot leveraging local contacts

Campaign Arc

  • Small political jobs → larger conspiracies
  • Player choices shape system alliances
  • Imperial response and Rebel interests escalate tension
